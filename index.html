Our Story

We are a group of college students unsatisfied with the options out there for fantasy soccer. So we decided to make our own!!! Welcome to Django's Fantasy the ultimate fantasy soccer website of all time. Enjoy!

Our Mission

Django's Fantasy is a user-friendly hybrid between fantasy leagues and traditional sports betting rules. Choose what you think are the best and worst teams in the league with our site! We are targeted toward both the experienced soccer fan and the novice fan. Our goal is to help burgining soccer fans get into fantasy soccer at an easy, entry level while still providing an exciting competition for experienced soccer fans. If you do not know much about Premier League Football don't fret!! We will give you advice on team picks so you can choose the best team even while first getting into the soccer world. Who will make the best prediction? Join with your friends now to find out!!!
