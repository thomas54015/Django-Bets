Our Story

We are a group of college students unsatisfied with the options out there for fantasy soccer. So we decided to make our own!!! Welcome to Django's Fantasy the ultimate fantasy soccer website of all time. Enjoy!

Our Mission

Django's Fantasy is a user-friendly fantasy league that is targeted towards both the experienced soccer fan and the novice fan. Our goal main goal is to help burgining soccer fans get into fantasy soccer. We will give you advice on team picks so you can build the best team even while first getting into the soccer world.
